Welcome to RECA Management System

An integrated platform for students, faculty members, HODs, librarians, and accountants

For Students

Our platform offers a range of features for students including:

  • View and manage your class schedule
  • Track attendance and view attendance history
  • Access sessional marks and academic performance
  • Stay updated with the latest notices
  • Update your profile and personal information
For Faculty

Our platform offers a range of features for faculty members including:

  • Manage class schedules and timetables
  • Record and track student attendance
  • Enter and update sessional marks
  • Post notices and announcements
  • Access and update personal profiles
For HODs

Our platform offers a range of features for HODs including:

  • Update class schedules and timetables
  • Add and update faculty information
  • Add and update subject information
  • Add students and manage student information
  • Perform other administrative tasks
For Librarians

Our platform offers a range of features for librarians including:

  • Calculate fines for overdue books
  • Manage library books and inventory
  • Add new books to the library
  • Issue and return books
  • Search for books and manage book records
For Accountants

Our platform offers a range of features for accountants including:

  • View fee details and payment information
  • Add new fee records
  • Search for fee records
  • Generate fee reports
  • Manage student fee payments
